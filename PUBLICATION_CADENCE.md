# Publication cadence

This is the repeatable publishing rhythm for Tillar Technologies.

The objective is not to go viral once.

The objective is to become legible, credible, and increasingly hard to ignore.

## Weekly cadence

### Monday — thesis

Publish one short post on:

- governance outside the model
- bounded control
- high-trust deployment
- why prompt-only safety is insufficient

### Wednesday — proof

Publish one artifact:

- repo update
- screenshot
- short demo clip
- paper excerpt
- lineage diagram

### Friday — founder / process

Publish one post on:

- why the work started
- what changed in your thinking
- what you shipped this week
- what problem you are trying to solve

## Monthly cadence

### Week 1

- launch or relaunch a proof surface

### Week 2

- publish one research or lineage artifact

### Week 3

- publish one demo or workflow clip

### Week 4

- publish one commercial / operator-facing framing post

## Topic rotation

Rotate through these six themes:

1. governance thesis
2. product-family lineage
3. CageOS Lite public proof
4. enterprise/high-trust relevance
5. founder build journey
6. research and papers

## Rules

- always point to one URL
- do not split attention across five links
- do not post private mechanics
- do not disappear for long gaps
- reuse the same thesis until the market starts repeating it back

## Success metric

The first goal is not follower count.

The first goal is:

- profile visits
- repo visits
- conversations
- startup program applications
- inbound serious interest

That is the operating metric set for this phase.
