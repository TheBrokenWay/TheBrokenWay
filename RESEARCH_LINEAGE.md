# Research lineage

This page connects the public papers, the product family, and the visible proof
surfaces.

It is designed for three readers:

- technical visitors who need the intellectual progression
- press or investors who need the company logic quickly
- the main implementation agent, who needs a compact map of the public story

## Short version

The lineage is:

1. foundational theory and mathematical framing
2. governance formulation for synthetic systems
3. invariant and operating-path formulation
4. domain application in PredX
5. governance abstraction into CageOS
6. public proof surface through CageOS Lite

## Stage 1: origin theory

Primary public-facing artifact:

- [35 Laws Math](publications/35_Laws_Math.pdf)

Role:

- establishes the earliest theory layer
- explains the pre-product conceptual frame
- documents the intellectual starting point before the systems split

## Stage 2: governance thesis

Primary artifact:

- [Constitutional Synthetic Agency](publications/Constitutional_Synthetic_Agency.pdf)

Role:

- converts the origin material into a governance-first thesis
- argues that synthetic systems need structural rules, not just model tuning
- introduces the idea that bounded operation must be enforced as part of the
  working path

## Stage 3: invariants and operations

Primary artifact:

- [The Archon Axiomatic System](publications/The_Archon_Axiomatic_System.pdf)

Role:

- translates governance into operational discipline
- emphasizes non-negotiable constraints and systems behavior
- points toward a substrate that can govern multiple downstream applications

## Stage 4: product split

At this point the work becomes modular.

### PredX

PredX keeps the original high-consequence domain pressure:

- scientific reasoning
- pharmaceutical validation
- evidence and decision support

This is the application domain that exposed the need for stronger governance in
the first place.

### CageOS

CageOS is the abstraction that came out of that pressure:

- governance outside the model
- bounded operating paths
- independent control logic
- reviewable allow / redirect / escalate / block outcomes

### CageOS Lite

CageOS Lite is the public proof surface:

- smaller scope
- visible demonstration
- no release of the private enforcement plane

## Why this lineage matters

Without this sequence, the public materials can look like separate ideas.

They are not separate ideas.

They are one chain:

- domain pressure created the need
- governance became the answer
- the governance substrate became valuable beyond the original domain

## Public-story rule

This lineage document is intentionally public-safe.

It explains:

- how the ideas connect
- why the product family exists
- why the public demo is small by design

It does not explain:

- internal enforcement mechanics
- deployment architecture details
- red-team handling
- sealed operating procedures
- trade-secret implementation paths
