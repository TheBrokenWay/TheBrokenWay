# Video scripts

These are short-form scripts for screen-recorded demos, founder videos, or
voiceover clips.

## Video 1 — 30-second thesis clip

Hook:

"Most teams are trying to improve the model. I’m building the governance layer
that tells the model what it is allowed to do."

Middle:

"That started as a scientific decision-support problem and became a broader
systems conclusion: in high-trust environments, the model cannot be its own
governor."

Close:

"That is the direction behind CageOS, PredX, and the public CageOS Lite Demo."

CTA:

"If you want the public proof surface, start on my GitHub."

## Video 2 — 45-second CageOS Lite walkthrough

Open on the demo repo or Pages site.

Script:

"This is CageOS Lite Demo. It is a deliberately limited public proof surface.

The point is not to publish every internal detail. The point is to prove one
claim clearly: governance can remain outside the model while still shaping what
the model is allowed to do.

The visible agent surface stays narrow. The independent governance layer
determines whether behavior is allowed, redirected, escalated, or blocked.

That is the pattern."

## Video 3 — founder origin clip

Script:

"This work did not start as a governance project. It started with a serious
scientific problem.

The more I worked on it, the clearer it became that better answers alone were
not enough. Prompting alone was not enough. Review after the fact was not
enough.

The real problem was governance in the operating path.

That is why the work split into PredX, CageOS, and CageOS Lite."

## Video 4 — enterprise relevance clip

Script:

"If an organization is going to use capable AI systems in a serious workflow,
then control cannot be advisory theater.

It has to stay in the operating path.

That is the difference between generic automation and governed systems."

## Recording guidance

- Use screen capture plus voiceover first. It is enough.
- Keep each clip under 60 seconds.
- End every clip with one URL only.
- Do not explain internals that are not already public.
